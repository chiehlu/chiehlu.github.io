Chieh Lu, PhD


Social and cultural psychologist | Web design learner | Writer

I am passionate about research on how individuals understand themselves, especially in relation to their sociocultural environment. I have studied the awareness of cultural influence on the self for years, and written my PhD dissertation on the development process of such awareness. Now I am open to research jobs that could either push my theoretical research forward or contribute to the real world.
